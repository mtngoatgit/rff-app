Riley's

our story

Riley Farms is now in its fourth generation of growing produce here in Utah. We distribute produce wholesale, in farmers' markets, and also have a storefront in Perry, Utah. Over the last two decades we have established a robust and growing produce delivery system, servicing office buildings and convenient stores.

since 1960

Riley farms began in Payson, Utah, where Howard Riley bought land and started growing apples. The Riley's now have farms in Goshen, Green River, and Perry (all in Utah). Howard's grandson, Alan oversees all operations, with two of his sons playing an active part in cultivation, production and distribution. The orchard in Perry is their newest project, with 160 acres of apple, peach, cherry, and apricot trees.
