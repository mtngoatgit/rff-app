Office Delivery

Fresh.Fast.Local.

For over 20 years, we have been delivering fruit to local businesses. Want a healthy snack option in your workplace? Contact us by phone or email, and you will have a basket fresh from our farm the very next week.

We source the items from our own local farms, as well as a network of farms with whom we have a relationship. This allows us to offer a large variety of produce, like pears, bananas, and pluots (plumb-apricots).

Schedule your deliveries for as often as you would like and alter your request of products as you needs change. Our fleet of drivers cover addresses from Payson to Logan.
